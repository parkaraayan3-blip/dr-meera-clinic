About

About Dr. Sharma

Dr. Meera Sharma is a board-certified dermatologist with over 18 years of experience in medical and cosmetic dermatology. After completing her MD in Dermatology from KEM Hospital, Mumbai, she pursued advanced training in laser dermatology in Singapore and South Korea.

She has worked as a consultant dermatologist at some of Mumbai's leading hospitals, including Hinduja Hospital, before founding Clear Skin Clinic in Bandra in 2018. Her work has been published in 12 peer-reviewed journals, with a special focus on pigmentation disorders in Indian skin.

Dr. Sharma believes in taking time with every patient, listening to their concerns, and creating treatment plans that are practical, affordable, and grounded in clinical evidence.

"Every patient is unique. I take time to understand your skin, your concerns, and your goals before recommending any treatment. There are no shortcuts in good dermatology."
